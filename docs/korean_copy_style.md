# Korean copy style

The Korean site uses polite but flexible public-facing prose.

- Explanatory sentences use polite formal endings.
- Questions may use natural polite endings.
- Headings, status labels, tags, and metadata remain concise noun phrases.
- Technical identifiers and project-specific English terms remain unchanged until a later terminology review.
- This copy change does not alter routes, research claims, trading boundaries, APIs, or execution behavior.
